About Us

Innovista Consortium is an educational initiative dedicated to shaping young minds through structured, beginner-friendly web development education. Our mission is to build confidence with technology while learning real-world digital skills.

Our Basic Coding Program

This program is specially designed for beginners with no prior programming experience.

  • HTML: Understanding web structure, tags, and page layout.
  • CSS: Styling web pages with colors, fonts, spacing, and layouts.
  • JavaScript (Basics): Adding simple interactivity such as buttons, alerts, and form behavior.
  • Mini Projects: Students build simple web pages to reinforce learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions schools and parents often ask.

+ Do students need prior coding experience?

No! The program is designed for complete beginners with zero coding background.

+ Do students need laptops?

Yes. A basic laptop or desktop computer is recommended for effective hands-on learning.

+ What if a school does not have enough laptops or has none at all?

Innovista Consortium has flexible teaching methods to include every school.

+ Is the program online or physical?

The program is primarily delivered physically (in-school). We visit partner schools to teach students ensuring hands-on guidance. However, online or hybrid sessions can be arranged for individuals.

+ How long is the program?

The program duration can be adjusted to fit a school's timetable, typically spanning several weeks.

+ Is the program suitable for all secondary school levels?

Yes! The program is designed for all Secondary School students. Lessons are adapted to suit different age groups and learning speeds.

+ How can our school partner with Innovista Consortium?

You can contact us via email or phone to discuss scheduling, structure, and partnership options.
